I am a Ph.D. student in School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University, supervised by Prof. Y. Charlie Hu. I am interested in Mobile Systems, Edge Computing, and their intersection with Machine Learning. My current research focuses on understanding and improving the performance of ML-powered AR systems. I received my B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2018.
